Your Trusted Partner in Fluid Handling Solutions

Since 1986, Cyclone Industries has been at the forefront of pump, valve and sealing system supply in South Africa. Based in Germiston, we import the highest-quality products from world-leading manufacturers and back every solution with expert engineering, after-sales service, and in-house CNC manufacturing.

  • In-house CNC Manufacturing & Repairs
  • Full-spectrum after-sales support
  • B-BBEE Level 3 certified
